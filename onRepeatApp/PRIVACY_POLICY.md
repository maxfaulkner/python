# Privacy Policy for onRepeat

**Effective date:** March 29, 2026

## Overview

onRepeat is a personal recipe manager for iPhone. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

**Short version: onRepeat collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device.**

---

## Information We Collect

onRepeat does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no internet connection, no user accounts, and no backend servers.

All data you enter — recipes, ingredients, tags, and grocery lists — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

When you use the recipe sharing feature, you choose to share a recipe by generating a link or QR code. The content of that link is encoded from your recipe data and shared only when you explicitly initiate the action through your device's share sheet. We do not receive, store, or have access to any recipes you share.

## Analytics and Tracking

onRepeat contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.

## Third-Party Services

onRepeat does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

## Children's Privacy

onRepeat does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your recipe data is stored on your device. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. We have no copy of your data and cannot delete it on your behalf.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, we will update the effective date above. Any future version of onRepeat that does collect data will require a new consent flow before that data is collected.
